About Intwine

Artists can sell merch, tickets and physical copies on the site along side their social media posts. We have a vision to make an all in one package for artists to brand themselves as well as a one stop platform for fans to get to know their artists in one unified space.

We are really excited for our users to discover new artists that they might love as well as finding out new things about artists that they already love.

This website is a work in progress, please be patient.
